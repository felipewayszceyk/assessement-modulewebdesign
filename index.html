Welcome To My Journey

I'm here to Help you!


I’m originally from Brazil and currently living in Ireland, where I’m building my life abroad while transitioning into a career in software Developer.

My journey hasn’t been a straight line. I first moved to Ireland in 2019, and after some time, I returned to Brazil in 2024. I spent two years there, but things didn’t turn out the way I expected. That experience gave me clarity — and the certainty that I wanted to build my future abroad. So I made the decision to come back to Ireland and start again.

Like many people, I’ve faced doubts, uncertainties, and challenges along the way. At the same time, I’ve been developing professionally, working toward entering the international job market and building my career.

Nothing about this process has been perfect and that’s exactly the reason why I chose to share it.

Here, you’ll find the reality of building a life abroad: the challenges, the lessons,and what truly matters when you decide to take this step.

If you’re thinking about moving abroad — or you’ve already started — you’ll probably see part of your own journey in mine.

🌍 About this blog

This is a space where I share my real experience living in Ireland — not just the good parts, but also the challenges that come with starting over in another country. The goal is to make things clearer for anyone thinking about doing the same.

✈️ Thinking about moving?

If Ireland is on your mind, take your time to understand how things work. The more prepared you are, the easier your experience will be.

🧠 What you’ll find here

In this blog, I’ll be sharing practical tips about visas, work, daily life, and also my personal journey — what worked, what didn’t, and what I would do differently.
